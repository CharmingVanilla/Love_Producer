          The Contestant Profile contains three sections: Basic Traits, Relationships, and
          Producer Trust.                                                         
                                                                                  
          Basic Traits represent the contestant’s core personality and generally remain
                                                                                  
          unchanged throughout the game. However, minor fluctuations may occur.   
          Relationships and Producer Trust are dynamic values. They change based on
          interactions between contestants and the player’s decisions as the producer. The
          producer can only see the color bar but there are detailed figures documented in
                                                                                  
          the system.

          Current  State                                                          
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
          Current State represents a contestant’s temporary emotional condition. It changes after
          important events and affects their next interaction or decision.        
                                                                                  
            ●  Calm: Emotionally stable and more likely to communicate normally and respond
                                                                                  
               rationally.                                                        
            ●  Upset: Hurt or unhappy, making the contestant more likely to withdraw, act coldly, or
               react negatively.                                                  
            ●  Jealous: Feels threatened when someone they like becomes close to another
                                                                                  
               contestant. They may confront, compete, or interfere.              
            ●  Embarrassed: Feels uncomfortable after rejection, failure, or an awkward moment.
               They may avoid others or become less proactive.                    
            ●  Excited: Feels positive after receiving a message, winning a challenge, or making
               romantic progress. They are more likely to interact and move a relationship forward.

          Determines how likely a contestant is to start conversations, ask someone on a date, send
          messages, confess their feelings, or actively move a relationship forward.
                                                                                  
          It reflects how proactive they are, not how quickly they fall in love.  
                                                                                  
          Sincerity                                                               
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
          Determines how genuine a contestant’s emotions and romantic intentions are.
                                                                                  
          It also affects how likely they are to perform for the cameras, hide their true feelings, or
          manipulate a relationship for attention. Higher Sincerity makes the audience more likely to
          believe their feelings are real.                                        
                                                                                  
          Emotional Stability                                                     
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
          Determines how a contestant reacts to rejection, jealousy, conflict, public criticism, and
          pressure from the show.                                                 
                                                                                  
          Contestants with low Emotional Stability are more likely to act impulsively, break down, or
          create conflict. Contestants with high Emotional Stability are better at staying calm and
                                                                                  
          handling difficult situations.                                          
                                                                                  
          Possessiveness                                                          
                                                                                  
          Determines how strongly a contestant reacts when someone they like becomes close to
          another person.                                                         
                                                                                  
          Higher Possessiveness increases the chance of jealousy, competition, controlling behavior,
                                                                                  
          and love-triangle conflict.                                             
                                                                                  
          Sociability                                                             
                                                                                  
          Determines how easily a contestant fits into the group, joins shared activities, and builds
          friendships with other contestants.                                     
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
          Show Sense                                                              
                                                                                  
          Determines how aware a contestant is of the cameras, public attention, and the rules of the
          show.                                                                   
                                                                                  
          Contestants with high Show Sense are better at creating memorable scenes, dramatic
                                                                                  
          moments, and discussion-worthy content. High Show Sense does not automatically mean low
          Sincerity.

          Relationship Chart                                                      
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
          Each contestant has a separate Relationship Chart for every other contestant. It contains three
          values: Attractiveness, Interest, and Trust.                            
                                                                                  
          Attractiveness                                                          
                                                                                  
          Represents how physically and personally appealing one contestant finds another.
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
          It is mainly influenced by:                                             
                                                                                  
            ●  Physical preferences                                               
            ●  Personal type preferences                                          
            ●  First impressions                                                  
            ●  Clothing and presentation                                          
                                                                                  
            ●  Date settings                                                      
            ●  Special events                                                     
                                                                                  
          Attractiveness is usually established early and changes more slowly than the other
          relationship values.                                                    
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
          Interest                                                                
                                                                                  
          Represents how strongly a contestant wants to know, pursue, or spend more time with another
          contestant.                                                             
                                                                                  
          It can be influenced by:                                                
                                                                                  
            ●  Daily conversations                                                
            ●  Private dates                                                      
                                                                                  
            ●  The other person’s initiative                                      
            ●  Romantic interactions                                              
            ●  Shared interests                                                   
            ●  New romantic competitors                                           
                                                                                  
            ●  Producer-created tasks and editing choices                         
                                                                                  
          Interest is usually the most frequently changing relationship value.

          Trust                                                                   
                                                                                  
          Represents whether a contestant sees another contestant as honest, reliable, and emotionally
          safe.                                                                   
                                                                                  
          It can be influenced by:                                                
                                                                                  
            ●  Keeping promises                                                   
                                                                                  
            ●  Sharing secrets                                                    
            ●  Providing emotional support                                        
            ●  Lying or hiding information                                        
            ●  Defending someone during conflict                                  
                                                                                  
            ●  Flirting with multiple people at the same time                     
                                                                                  
          Trust affects deep conversations, secret-sharing, confession success, and final relationship
          outcomes.                                                               
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
          Producer  Trust                                                         
                                                                                  
          Each contestant has a separate Producer Trust value, representing how much they trust the
                                                                                  
          player and the production team. Producer Trust changes throughout the season and affects
          how willing a contestant is to cooperate with the player’s decisions.   
                                                                                  
          Producer Trust increases when the player:                               
                                                                                  
            ●  Respects the contestant’s choices and personal boundaries          
            ●  Treats contestants fairly                                          
                                                                                  
            ●  Arranges dates and activities that match their preferences         
            ●  Gives fair and helpful interview guidance                          
            ●  Keeps promises                                                     
            ●  Supports them when their stress is high                            
            ●  Protects them from unfair or harmful situations                    
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
          Producer Trust decreases when the player:                               
                                                                                  
            ●  Repeatedly forces unwanted interactions                            
            ●  Blocks or interferes with their messages                           
            ●  Separates them from someone they genuinely like                    
            ●  Uses misleading or harmful editing                                 
                                                                                  
            ●  Reveals private information                                        
            ●  Pushes them into conflict for entertainment                        
            ●  Clearly favors a specific contestant or couple                     
            ●  Ignores their discomfort or personal boundaries                    
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                                                                                  

===== PAGE 6 =====
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
          Contestants with high Producer Trust are more likely to:                
                                                                                  
            ●  Reveal their true feelings during interviews                       
            ●  Accept the player’s guidance                                       
            ●  Cooperate with arranged conversations and activities               
            ●  Stay longer in interactions created by the player                  
                                                                                  
            ●  Tolerate minor interventions                                       
                                                                                  
          Contestants with low Producer Trust may:                                
                                                                                  
            ●  Hide their true feelings                                           
            ●  Refuse the player’s instructions                                   
            ●  Leave forced conversations early                                   
                                                                                  
            ●  Return to their previous location after being moved                
            ●  Ignore the player’s preferred pairing                              
            ●  Act against the player’s plans                                     
                                                                                  
          This creates a balance between intervention and fairness. The player needs to influence
                                                                                  
          relationships to create an entertaining show, but excessive control can make contestants stop
          cooperating.
